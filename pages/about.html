Our Story

"Bringing the authentic taste of Africa to the world, one meal at a time."

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010, Giant Afro Foods began as a small family-owned business in Lagos, Nigeria. Our founder, Adunola Adebayo, noticed the growing demand for high-quality African food products in the diaspora. What started as a local market stall has now grown into an international brand supplying fresh and frozen African foods worldwide.

We take pride in preserving traditional African recipes while incorporating modern food safety standards. Every product we deliver carries the richness of African heritage.
